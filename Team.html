Team

The GA-NIFS collaboration includes 37 members from 21 institutions, across 9 countries.
The P.Is of the survey are Roberto Maiolino and Santiago Arribas; the team is currently coordinated by Hannah Übler and Michele Perna.

Group picture taken during the GA-NIFS collaboration meeting in Arcetri (2023).
